Welcome! I decided to host my writing here, on my own site, rather than on a third-party platform.

The reasons are simple: I own the content and the URLs, the writing strengthens my own portfolio rather than someone else's platform, and I get full control over how technical content — code blocks, math, diagrams — actually renders.

## What to expect

I'll mostly write about the things I work on: applied AI and machine learning, data and ETL engineering, and the occasional note on software engineering practice. Posts are written in plain Markdown, so expect clean prose, real code samples, and the odd equation.

Thanks for reading — more soon.
